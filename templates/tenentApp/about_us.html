Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a property?

Uploading a property is simple. Navigate to the "Upload Property" section in your dashboard, fill out the details (title, price, images), and submit. Your listing will be live after a quick review.

Can tenants apply online?

Yes! Tenants can browse properties and submit applications directly through the platform. You can track all applications in your dashboard.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. Your privacy is our top priority.

How do I manage rental agreements?

Once drafted, agreements can be viewed and signed digitally by both parties. Signed documents are securely stored in your profile for easy access.
